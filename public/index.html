How to change WhatsApp full profile picture?
▾
Upload your photo on this page, enter your WhatsApp number with country code, get a pair code, and enter it in WhatsApp under Settings → Linked Devices. Your profile picture will be updated automatically to full size.
Is my WhatsApp account safe?
▾
Yes, completely safe. We only link temporarily to change your DP and then immediately log out and delete all session data. We never store your credentials or messages.
Why do I need to enter a pair code?
▾
The pair code is WhatsApp's official way to link a device without scanning a QR code. It's the same method WhatsApp uses for WhatsApp Web. We use it to temporarily access your account just to change your profile picture.
How long does it take?

The entire process takes less than 60 seconds. Upload your photo, enter your number, get a code in seconds, enter it in WhatsApp, and your profile picture is changed instantly.
